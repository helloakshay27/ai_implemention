Feature Description
Splash Screen
A Splash Screen Is An Initial Screen That Shows An App’s Logo Or Branding While The App Launches, Displaying The Splash Screen. Duration– Splash Screen Typically Lasts 2-3 Seconds.
Access Screen
The User Is Taken To The Welcome Screen Or Access Screen With Three Main Actions To Perform: 1. Login (For Existing Users > Post Sales) 2. As A Guest (Explore Property Portfolio Without Registration)
Onboarding Screens
An Onboarding Screen Guides New Users Through Key Features And Setup Steps When They First Use An App.
1st Lower Navigation CTA : Home
Hamburger Menu (More Options)
A Hamburger Menu (☰) Is A Button That Opens A Hidden Menu. For Pre-Sales, It Can Include Options Like My Enquiries, My Favorites, Customer Support , FAQs, & Contact Info.
Registration
A Registration For A Pre-Sale App Is A Process Where User Registers To Explore Properties And Avail Functionalities Like Enquire, Refer Now, Favourite Etc. It Usually Requires Basic Details Like Name, Email & Mobile Number
My Enquiries
My Enquiries In A Pre-Sale App Is A Feature Where Users Can Track Their Enquiries About Projects.
My Site Visit
The My Site Visit Feature Allows Users To Easily Schedule And Manage Property Visits, Select A Preferred Time & Date, And Receive Reminders Or Confirmations About Their Appointment. It Streamlines The Process Of Arranging Site Visits For Sale Properties.
My Favorite
My Favorite Feature In An App Lets Users Mark As Favorite And Access Properties They Are Interested In, Making It Easier To Enquire Or Revisit Their Top Choices.
My Referral
My Referral Is A Section Where Users Can View The List Of Referrals He/She Made During The Exploration Journey
FAQs
FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions) Are A List Of Common Questions And Answers To Help Users Quickly Find Information About A Product, Service, Or Process.
Contact Us
The Contact Us Feature In A Pre-Sale App Allows Users To Reach Out To The Support Team For Help With Questions, Product Details, Or Issues. User Can Put In Basic Personal Details Along With A Message And Submit The Enquiry To Be Contacted By Sales Executive.
Chat with us

Terms & conditions
Terms and Conditions in a pre-sale outline the rules, guidelines, and policies that customers must agree to when participating in the pre-sale. They cover eligibility, payment, refunds, and other important details.
Privacy policy
A Privacy Policy in a pre-sale app explains how customer data is collected, used, stored, and protected. It informs users about their privacy rights and how their personal information will be handled during the pre-sale process.
News feed
A News Feed in a pre-sale app displays social media tabs, blogs, & important news.
Notification preference
The Notification Preference feature allows users to customize how and when they receive notifications, such as alerts about product updates, offers, through methods like email, SMS, or in-app messages
Theme
The Theme feature allows users to customize the visual appearance of the app, such as selecting between light or dark modes.
Exit
The Exit feature in a pre-sale app allows users to safely redirect user to the access screen
Search bar
A Search Bar in a allows users to quickly find Residential properties, Commercial Properties or plots by typing keywords, making it easier to navigate the app and access specific content. Based on quick filters user can easily find properties according to his interest.
Notification/bell icon
A Real-Time Notification in a pre-sale app provides instant alerts to users about important updates, such as new Property launches, Loyalty programs, referral programs etc
Chat Bot
The Chat feature allows users to communicate directly with a support bot in real time for assistance with enquiries, questions, or concerns related to the propert portfolio or other services
Project banner
Project Banners are visual graphics or images displayed in a sale app to promote specific projects, products, referral program or offers. They usually appear at the top or in prominent areas to grab users attention and highlight key information.
Featured
A featured feature in an application refers to a highlighted project which is a major selling point property. It is often a unique or highly valuable aspect of any application that sets it apart from competitors, meets a key user need, or is heavily promoted in marketing efforts.

Panchshil privilege
The Panchshil Privilege feature in a sale app offers exclusive perks like early access to offers, priority booking, discounts, and personalized services for registered or loyal customers, enhancing their pre-sale experience.
Testimonials
Testimonials in a sale app are reviews or positive feedback from customers about products or services. They help build trust and credibility by sharing real user experiences with others considering the sale offer

2D image (CTA)
A 2D image is a flat visual representation that has only height and width, with no depth or perspective. Examples include , floor plans photographs, drawings, and digital graphics displayed on screens or paper.
virtual tour (CTA)
A Virtual Tour is an online experience that allows users to explore a space remotely, typically using 360-degree images or videos. It helps users view properties or locations from any distance, making it especially useful in real estate or tourism apps.
video (CTA)
The Video feature in a project detail section allows users to watch videos related to the project.
Map (CTA)
The Project Map feature in a sale app shows a visual representation of the project s location, layout, and nearby areas. It helps users understand the property s surroundings.
Overview
An Overview in a project detail section is a brief summary that descriptions ,project details, . It provides essential information to help users quickly understand what the project is about before exploring more detailed aspects.
Gallery
A Gallery in a sale app showcases images, or other media related to the products or properties on sale. It allows users to visually explore options, such as floor plans, property views, or product features, helping them make more informed decisions before purchasing.
brochure
A brochure in a panchshil app is a digital document that provides detailed information about projects or properties, including features, floor plans, pricing, and images. It helps users understand what is being offered before making a decision, often available for viewing or download within the app.
Enquire (CTA)
The Enquire feature in a sale app allows users to ask questions or request more details about products or services. It typically involves submitting a form or reaching out to the sales team for additional information.
refer (CTA)
The Refer feature in a pre-sale app allows users to share referral with others. When someone uses the referral, both the referrer and the referee may receive rewards like discounts or exclusive offers. It encourages users to promote the app to their friends and expand its user base.
site visit (cTA)
The Site Visit feature in a Panchshil app allows users to schedule and manage property visits, select a date and time, and confirmations. It streamlines the process of visiting properties, making it easier for users to plan and track their appointments.
